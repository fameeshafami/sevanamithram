Who We Are

Sevanamithram Public Service Center is a trusted community service centre in Balussery, providing government and digital services with simplicity, accuracy, and care.

As an Authorized eMithram Franchise, we deliver secure and reliable public services while offering dedicated support to Pravasi and NRI families through PravasiMithram and PravasaLokam.

Saving your time. Simplifying every service. Serving with trust.

About Us

One trusted center for all your public service needs.

Sevanamithram is more than an ordinary online service center. We bring together government services, digital solutions, and dedicated Pravasi support through three specialized service brands—all under one trusted platform.

SEVANAMITHRAM

Public Service Center

Government services, certificates, PAN, Aadhaar, passport assistance, utility payments, documentation, and a wide range of public services.

eMithram Franchise

All online government and digital services are delivered through our Authorized eMithram Franchise, ensuring secure, reliable, and efficient access to official government platforms.

PRAVASALOKAM

Dedicated support for NRIs and Pravasi families including NORKA services, documentation, passport guidance, embassy assistance, and travel support.

SPRAC

Sevanamithram Pravasi Assistance & Resource Centre

Assistance for employment concerns, welfare matters, petitions, embassy communication, documentation, and community support for expatriates.

  • Authorized eMithram franchise center
  • Secure and verified document handling
  • Clear guidance at every step
  • Dedicated Pravasi & NRI support desk
